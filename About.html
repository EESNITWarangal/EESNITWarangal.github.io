WHO ARE WE?

The Electrical Engineering Society (EES) is a vibrant and active organization dedicated to enriching the lives of electrical engineering students through a blend of technical, cultural, and sports-related activities. It acts as a platform where students can explore their interests, collaborate with peers, and showcase their talents.

The society organizes a range of events throughout the year, from technical workshops to cultural events and sports tournaments, ensuring a balance between academic and extracurricular pursuits. Its events often attract participation from students, fostering a spirit of camaraderie and teamwork.

EES also plays a pivotal role in bridging the gap between academics and industry. Guest lectures, industrial visits, and alumni interactions provide students with insights into real-world applications of their studies, inspiring innovation and career growth. At the same time, cultural and sports activities promote creativity, fitness, and teamwork, making it a well-rounded platform for holistic development.
